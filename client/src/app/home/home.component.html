Civic Smart City

Community-centered approach to questioning and discovering the value of urban technologies

Civic Smart City seeks to create and promote value sensitive approaches to identifying and implementing urban technologies. It explores new modes of public engagement, media literacy and shared governance that are necessary for creating equitable and just smart cities.

Guiding Values

These five plays are actions that can be taken by the range of actors involved in the design and implementation of the smart city, prioritizing a values-first approach that can be implemented across organizations.
